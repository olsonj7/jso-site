Linkside SMS Consent

Linkside is a golf tee-time coordination app operated by JSO Solutions. This page describes how users opt in to receive SMS messages from Linkside.

Message program purpose: transactional and service-related text messages for account verification, golf round invitations, RSVP updates, tee-time schedule changes, open-spot notifications, and other service-related notifications.

How users opt in

Users opt in directly inside the Linkside mobile app during signup.

  1. The user opens the Linkside app and enters their mobile phone number.
  2. The user is shown SMS disclosure language and links to the Privacy Policy and Terms and Conditions.
  3. The user checks the consent box agreeing to receive SMS messages from Linkside.
  4. The user taps continue.
  5. The user receives a one-time verification code by SMS and enters it in the app to complete signup.

Consent disclosure shown at signup

By checking this box and continuing, you agree to receive SMS messages from Linkside, a product of JSO Solutions, for account verification, golf round invitations, RSVP updates, tee-time changes, open-spot notifications, and other service-related notifications. Message frequency varies. Message and data rates may apply. Reply STOP to opt out and HELP for help. Privacy Policy and Terms and Conditions.

Sample message types

  • Account verification codes
  • Golf round invitations
  • RSVP confirmations
  • Tee-time updates or schedule changes
  • Open-spot notifications related to an existing round

Sample SMS messages

Verification:
Linkside: Your verification code is 123456. It expires in 10 minutes. Msg & data rates may apply.

Invitation:
Linkside: You're invited to a golf round at Rush Creek on Sat at 8:10 AM. Reply YES, NO, or MAYBE. Msg frequency varies. Msg & data rates may apply. Reply STOP to opt out, HELP for help.

Update:
Linkside: The Rush Creek tee time moved to 8:20 AM on Sat. Reply YES, NO, or MAYBE. Msg frequency varies. Msg & data rates may apply. STOP to opt out, HELP for help.

Opt-out and help

Users may opt out at any time by replying STOP. Users may request help by replying HELP.

Opt-out response:
Linkside: You have successfully unsubscribed and will no longer receive messages. Reply START to resubscribe.

Help response:
Linkside: Golf tee-time and RSVP notifications. Message frequency varies. Msg & data rates may apply. Reply STOP to opt out. Support: [YOUR SUPPORT EMAIL]
